    STORY
    I joined Smart Joules Pvt. Ltd. as an embedded system intern and among the first developers in the company during my 8th semester of undergrad. Later, joined by 2 more developers, we collaboratively built a Smart Energy Management System, an end-to-end solution, which started as an idea on a piece of paper and is today deployed across 15+ sites pan India reducing energy consumption by 40% daily and saving energy equivalent to electrifying 40 households for a year.
    The Product
    Dejoule- Smart Energy Management System when deployed in a building,is capable of deriving energy savings leveraging the pool of IoT edge controllers deployed across the facility. These pool of IoT controllers sense data about to energy utilization ()across the building from HVAC components, electrical and electronic appliances, sends it to the cloud for monitoring and Analysis, recieves remote command to optimally control these energy-draining pieces of equipment to avoid wastage. These edge controllers are also capable to running a plethora of algorithms like PID and recipes/logics(Joule Recipes-tool developed by the team to develop and deploy energy-savings logics onto the edge) leveraging distributed computing.
    My Contribution
    As a embedded system intern, I worked along with three other developers, brainstorming the architecture design, developing core services responsible for monitoring and control on the edge devices which constitutes of reading data from the sensors, interfacing controllers with the HVAC components like chillers, VFDs and energy meters, developing the IoT communication pipeline etc. After the internship period, I worked on developing other essential services like the logging,over-the-air code deployment pipeline etc. and then deploying these edge controllers on different sites pan-India. I am currently leading a team of talented,motivated and determined people on shifting entire centralized edge IoT architecture to decentralized architecture with a motive of removing architecture design with flaws like single point of failures, scaling the architecture etc. Collaboratively, worked on developing and deploying energy saving services and tools like Joule recipes on the edge, optimizing the code and services for better performance in minimum resources with distributed computing.

    Secured Plug-N-Play IoT Framework

    This project provides a framework to set up an IoT communication pipeline which will provide support for different IoT communication protocols and encryption/decryption standards. The idea behind this framework is to provide a plug and play platform for edge controllers to leverage this IoT infrastructure for communications. The framework has the support to communicate over WiFi, LoraWAN, and Zigbee. Edge controllers preferring to communicate over wifi will have the support to either of these communication protocols:
    MQTT
    AMQP
    The edge controllers can also communicate over LoraWAN and Zigbee using Lora and Zigbee protocols respectively. The framework also provides support to encrypt packets during communication with different encryption standards like:

    AES: Advanced Encryption Standard is one of the most commonly used and preferred encryption algorithm. This farmwork supports 128/192/256 bit encryption.

    Speck and Simon: This encryption algorithm was developed by NSA and is one of the most lightweight encryption algorithms for IoT, capable of encrypting packets utilizing limited memory, requires low-energy and has high throughput which makes it ideal for embedded devices. Fig 1 describes the architecture and workflow of the framework. All the above-mentioned preferences could be configured using a configuration file. This framework supports embedded c and python.
